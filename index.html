HOW DOES IT WORK?

Every data extraction project is different and solution needs to be designed to exact requirements.  Our Data as a Service solution delivers data exactly the way you want, at the frequency you want, in the manner you want. 

Our Process

STEP 1: SUBMIT REQUIREMENTS

Submit requirements on data sources and feeds, websites to track, fields to extract and frequency of extraction.

STEP 2: RECEIVE SAMPLE DATA

According to the requirements, we provide sample data extracted by our models. You validate the data or suggest changes.

STEP 3: MODEL FINALIZATION

We finalize the models and web crawlers, data transformations and data access methods.

STEP 4: DATA ACCESS

You access the data in your preferred way: download excel files or store in your preferred cloud or internal storage provider.
