Report Sustainability With Ease.

Sustainability Report Online enables you to report ESG information about your company, in accordance with GRI and other international frameworks, that your customers or other stakeholders request from you. Through an intuitive guided tour, you can create and file reports with the standards agencies, or make them conveniently available in our global repository, making it accessible to anyone interested in your information.

Reporting

Sustainability reporting begins with collecting accurate and reliable data. Our smart system has several checks for discrepancies and irregularities to alert you so that your reports are high on accuracy and consistency, always up to date and investment-grade.

Benchmarking

Customers, Regulators and Investors compare your company with your peers. Our global benchmarking tools allow you to customize the peer universe to give you perspective so that you always know where you stand relative to peers.

Publishing

Publish beautiful reports to your website and update them year-round. File GRI, CDP, GRESB, UN SDG or several other reports all through your corporate account. Reuse your data entered once for a report and Sustainability Report Online quickly creates multiple accurate, complete reports.
